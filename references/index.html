  • Information System Mapping

Information System (IS) mapping is an essential tool for mastering your information system (IS), and is mandatory for Operators of Vital Importance (OIV). Mapping your IS enables you to know all the elements that make it up, so as to gain a better understanding of it, and therefore better control over it. It forms part of an overall risk management approach.
